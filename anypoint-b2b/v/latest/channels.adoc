= Channels

A _channel_ provides a means of specifying, for a given partner:

Type:: Source or Target
Document:: As created in link:/anypoint-b2b/document-types[Document Types]
Document Map:: As created in link:/anypoint-b2b/maps[Maps]
Endpoint:: As created in link:/anypoint-b2b/endpoints[Endpoints]

include::/_sources/edit-settings.adoc[]
[start=3]

. In the *CONFIGURATION* section of the left-hand navigation pane on the <<partner-configuration.adoc#img-company-information, Company Information Page>>, click *Channels*.
+
The <<img-channels>> appears.

[[img-channels]]

image::channels.png[img-channels, title="Channels Page"]

. From this page, you can:

* <<Edit an Existing Channel>>
* <<Add a New Channel>>

== Edit an Existing Channel

. On the <<img-channels>>, click the channel you want to edit.
+
The <<img-channel>> appears.
